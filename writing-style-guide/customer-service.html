Customer Service

Social media, Itunes and Google Play are popular forums for TrueCar customers to voice their concerns.

The old adage "the customer is always right" is might handy when communicating with TrueCar customers. Follow these tenets of civil correspondence when dealing with any customer concerns.

Listen First

You can't help a customer through their problem unless you know what that problem is. Read through their entire complaint, then reach out to them apologizing that they didn't have a good experience.

Always be sure that you understand the issue they are facing before you make any claims.

Practice Empathy

Customer service copywriting is all about practicing empathy.

Always make a genuine effort to let the customer know that you care, and that you are taking their concerns and complaints seriously.

Never Argue

The easiest way to turn a customer service engagement sour is to get angry, defensive or irritated with the Customer.

The most important thing to keep in mind is to always try and see things from the customer's point of view. If a customer is complaining about TrueCar in public forum, then the chances are very good that they are already irritated to begin with. No use making it worse.

Send them to CS

It's best not to try and drag out a conversation on social media or in an app store. The most effective way to solve a customer service issue is to direct it to our excellent customer service department (888) 878-3227.

Message the number to the user and ask if they can give us a call.
