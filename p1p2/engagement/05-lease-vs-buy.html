05 Lease vs. Buy - USAA Version, V1 - Crafted 1092017 - Pre-Approval

Dear Mr. Farrell

Thank you for using the USAA Car Buying Service, powered by TRUECar.

We bet you're pretty excited to go car shopping; we won't get in your way. But first, here's some information on how it all works.

Step 1. Confirm Your Membership

USAA members save on average $3,335 off of MSRP when compared to non-members.

These savings and incentives are available only to USAA members, so be sure to have your membership number at the ready when it's time to visit the dealership.

Step 2. Contact a Dealership

We value your privacy, and unlike most other car-buying services, we do not automatically send your contact information to the dealer.

You can control how dealers contact you from within your account, and dealer contact happens when only you initiate it.

Step 3. Redeem Your Offer

Once you make contact with a dealership, your offer details— attached to your USAA membership account—will be sent to your email address for safekeeping.

Be sure to print this out and bring it to the dealership with you to ensure you get your savings.

That's all for now, happy car shopping.

Thank you,

USAA
