02 Trade-in Value - USAA Version
V1. DIY Car Improvement - V1 Crafted 1092017 - Pre-Approval

Dear Mr. Farrell,

Now that you're shopping for a new vehicle, it’s important that you know the value of your trade-in so you can apply it towards the purchase of your new car or truck.

We have a few tips to maximize the value of your used car, so you can get the most out of it when it's time to trade-up.

Get it detailed

A professional detailing service can have a car with minor wear and tear looking almost new. If you're the DIY type, you can tackle this task yourself if you've got the time.

Get it serviced

An oil change, a tire rotation plus all the little ins and outs of routine car maintenance can add up to a more valuable trade-in when it's time to turn in the keys.

Once you've covered the basics, you can use our trade-estimate tool to find the actual residual value of your car.
