03 Credit Score - USAA Version

Dear Mr. Farrell,

Whether you are looking to buy, lease or finance a vehicle, your credit score has a huge impact on how much you end up paying. Your credit score can range from 300 to 850, and the higher the score numerically, the better.

The top three factors that affect your credit score are:

1. Credit Payment History: Creditors like to know that you make your payments on time.

2. Length of Credit History: The longer you've had a positive credit history, the better.

3. Amount of Credit Owed: The total sum owed to all of your creditors—inlcuding home and auto loans.

If you're not sure of your credit history, or where you stand credit-wise, you can use USAA's free CreditCheck tool and find out today.

Once you know your credit history, we can help you secure an Auto Loan through—and find the best price on a vehicle—all through USAA.
