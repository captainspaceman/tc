What is Residual Value?

Residual value is the total value of your car or truck—taking into account overall quality, durability, and brand desirability—calculated as a percentage of the original MSRP.

// example stack //

MSRP: $25,000

Residual Value: 50%

Length of Lease: 36 months

Residual value at the end of the lease: $12,500

Whether you're looking to lease, buy, trade or sell, the residual value is a huge factor in the price of a car.
