But with terms like MSRP, invoice, Dealer price, it’s hard to make sense of all the numbers. So what happens? You end up, on your own, digging around to see where those prices came from, or if they’re even fair.

Not anymore. Introducing TruePrice from TrueCar. Now, you can know exactly what you’ll pay for the car you want, including fees and accessories, before you even get to the Dealership. That’s right. Just go to TrueCar.com to see the TruePrice on cars like the one you want, all from the comfort of home.

So, how will you know if your TruePrice is fair? Because the first thing TrueCar does is show you what other people paid for the car you want, so you can recognize a great price. And your Certified Dealers know this, so they set their TruePrice competitively so they can win your business.
