Advertiser code of conduct policy

At TrueCar our mission is to be open, transparent and trustworthy throughout our entire brand experience.

We achieve this by being respectful of everyone regardless of race, nationality, gender, sexual orientation or religious beliefs.

We expect the advertisers we partner with to have the same philosophy.

Accordingly, if your content contains one or more of the following prohibited "tones" we (insert legalese sounding repercussion.)

Intolerant

Content that is hateful or designed to promote bias or hatred of a group of people

  • Racism of any kind
  • homophobic or transphobic speech
  • Religious intolerance
  • Sexism or exploitative speech about another sex

Shocking

Content designed to disgust or horrify viewers without providing any source of context or news value

  • Gratuitous violence
  • Content designed to influence people to harm themselves or others
  • Animal cruelty

False

Content purporting to be News that is objectively false but is designed to "trick" readers into thinking it is real.

Note: this rule does not apply to satire websites who clearly label themselves as such. Example: The Onion.

Nor does this rule apply to reputable website that also offer editorials which express opinions that may be false. Example: The New York Times Opinion Section.

Exploitative

Content that is exploitative in nature or designed to exploit others

  • Sexual exploitation
  • Exploitation of minors
  • Exploitation of disenfranchised or subjugated classes of people

Illegal

Content created to profit from, or promote an illegal business or enterprise.
