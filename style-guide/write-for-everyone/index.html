Write for Everyone


Summary: Simpler is better. Don't use unnecessary words or phrases. Write as you talk.


TrueCar users do not fall into any one bucket. They come from all walks of life and have all manner of educational backgrounds. Every visitor should be able to understand the value TrueCar brings to the car buying process. The tone of TrueCar product copy is essential in making this understanding possible.

In other words: write as if you were explaining TrueCar to one of your relatives at a family gathering.

"It's a great way to buy a car or truck."

"It's the best way to buy a vehicle."
